Codes

Through an extremely tedious UI experience, you can put codes into the Tamagotchi Uni and get special items! Certain things like the "DLC" are exclusive with limited use codes.

If the item has a code in this list, you can download it!

Events

Monthly

  • 5th of each month Tamagotchi digs hole in backyard and finds Gotchi Points
  • 5th, 15th, and 25th of each month 50% off Tama Delivery
  • 10th, 20th, and 30th of each month 50% off Tama Mall
  • 15th of even number months A ghost appears in the living room
  • 19th of each month A group of Shirotamas serenade the Tamagotchi in the backyard
  • 25th of odd number months Mittsupotchi uses the Tamagotchi's toilet and pays them 10 Gotchi Points

Specific Dates

  • Jan 1:New Year's gift
  • March 31 - April 4:Usatchi arrives in house with Easter Eggs
  • September 15th If the Tamagotchi visits the backyard after 7 PM, they will make some mochi to share with the moon who is hiding
  • November 23rd The Tamagotchi visits Gotchi King Castle to celebrate the Tamagotchi franchise's birthday
  • December 24th Santaclautchi and Rednosetchi arrive to deliver presents
  • December 25th Tamagotchi opens gift from Santaclautchi
  • User's birthday The Tamagotchi wishes the user happy birthday and gives them a cake
