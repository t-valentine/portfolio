Reading List

Books

I read a lot. I read 50 books in 2022. Since then, I'm taking it slow and am just reading one or two books a month. I like Scifi/Fantasy, anything weird, and (most of all) a huge terrible tome to slog through. I've actually spent 4 years trying to read Moby Dick, at this point it feels like finishing it may result in my untimely death. Currently I'm reading The Book of the New Sun by Gene Wolfe for the first time. I'm sure I will immediately reread it upon finishing the final book, it seems like that kind of series.
