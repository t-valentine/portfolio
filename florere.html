Thousands of years ago, six celestial beings bestowed magic and science onto the citizens of a small planet named FLORERE. Emboldened by their newfound abilities, the people of FLORERE explored the known universe!

Over time the people began to lose their way and forget important lessons taught by the celestial beings. The people forgot so much they even lost the map to the planet FLORERE. Knowing people would become over-zealous traversing space, THE HUNTER left his will in the hands of the wizards.

It's said that the heir to the WILL OF VENATOR can decipher the WILL and lead people back to
FLORERE!
